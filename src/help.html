Help Document for users

Slides and videos page 
Learning step:
1.	Using slides for self-learning. Click arrow at side of slide to toggle slide.
2.	Watch the teaching videos to get a deeper understanding of slides.
3.	Look code demo to learn how to write standard code.

Code-test online page 
How to use:
1.	Write your code on black text box above
2.	Checking carefully, then click run
3.	The results will display on the command line below
Test and Coursework 
How to use:
1.	Click title on the top to choose chapter
2.	Read content of coursework
3.	Click online-code button to do them immediately or upload button to upload your code file
Discussion board 
How to use:
1.	Write down your comment in the text box
2.	Click submit button to submit
My group 
How to invite your teammate:
1.	Click person icon on the right top to log in
2.	Click invite button to invite your teammate
ASSESSMENT 
How to contact with your teacher:
1.	Move cursor to figure of teacher
2.	Then you can contact them by using facebook, twitter or other way

How to register:

2.	After entering the registration information, click the LOGIN button
Search 
How to search:
1.	Enter the chapter you want to search in the search box.
2.	In the list that appears, select the chapter page you want to jump to
